# Answers — Lesson 33: Future Tense will
## उत्तर — पाठ 32

---

### Exercise 1 Answers | उत्तर

Tomorrow I **will go** to school.

She **will come** tomorrow.

We **will play** together.

They **will drink** water.

I **will read** a book.

---

### Exercise 2 Answers | उत्तर

Tomorrow I **will eat** rice.

Tomorrow she **will go** to school.

Tomorrow they **will play** cricket.

Tomorrow he **will read** a book.

---

### Rule Reminder | नियम

- **will** is the same for ALL subjects → I will, he will, she will, we will, they will
- After **will** → always use the base verb (eat, go, play — NOT eats, goes, plays)
