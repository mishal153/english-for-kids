# Answers — Lesson 27: Question Words Where / When / How / Why
## उत्तर — पाठ 26

---

### Exercise 1 Answers | उत्तर

```
तुम कहाँ रहते हो?  →  Where do you live?
वह कब आएगा?        →  When will he come?
तुम कैसे हो?        →  How are you?
तुम उदास क्यों हो?  →  Why are you sad?
```

### Exercise 2 Answers | उत्तर

**Where** is the school?

**When** do you come to school?

**How** are you?

**Why** is the sky blue?

**Where** is your district?

**How** do you submit this form — online or offline?
