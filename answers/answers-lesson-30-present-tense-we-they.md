# Answers — Lesson 30: Present Tense We / They
## उत्तर — पाठ 29

---

### Exercise 1 Answers | उत्तर

We **go** to school.

They **play** cricket.

We **read** books.

They **drink** water.

We **live** here.

They **eat** food.

---

### Exercise 2 Answers | उत्तर

My friends **play** football.

We **go** to school together.

They **eat** lunch at noon.

She **reads** every night.

We **live** near the market.

---

### Rule Summary | नियम

| Subject | Verb |
|---------|------|
| I / You / We / They | eat, go, play (no -s) |
| He / She / It | eats, goes, plays (add -s) |
