# Answers — Lesson 30: Past Tense was / were
## उत्तर — पाठ 30

---

### Exercise 1 Answers | उत्तर

Yesterday I **was** sick.

He **was** happy yesterday.

We **were** at school yesterday.

They **were** outside yesterday.

She **was** smart.

The sky **was** clear.

---

### Exercise 2 Answers | उत्तर

Yesterday I **was** happy.

Yesterday he **was** tired.

Yesterday they **were** in school.

She **was** my friend.

Yesterday we **were** late.
