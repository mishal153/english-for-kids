---

### Exercise 1 Answers | उत्तर

```
क्लिक करो   →  Click
शेयर करो   →  Share
डाउनलोड करो →  Download
हटाओ       →  Delete
सेव करो    →  Save
```

### Exercise 2 Answers | उत्तर

To send a photo to your friend, **share** it.

To keep a file on your phone, **download** it.

To keep your work safe, **save** it.

To remove a wrong file, **delete** it.

To purchase something online, click **Buy**.

After filling the form, click **Submit**.
