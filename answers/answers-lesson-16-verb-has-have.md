# Answers — Lesson 16: Verbs has / have
## उत्तर — पाठ 16

---

### Exercise 2 Answers | उत्तर

I **have** a book.

He **has** a dog.

We **have** two pens.

She **has** long hair.

They **have** a big house.

What do you **have**?

---

### Rule Reminder | नियम याद रखो

- Use **has** with → He, She, It (and names: Priya, Ram...)
- Use **have** with → I, You, We, They
