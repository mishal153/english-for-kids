# Answers — Lesson 32: Past Tense went / did / ate
## उत्तर — पाठ 31

---

### Exercise 1 Answers | उत्तर

```
go    →  went
eat   →  ate
drink →  drank
play  →  played
read  →  read
```

### Exercise 2 Answers | उत्तर

Yesterday I **went** to school.

She **ate** food.

We **drank** water.

They **played** cricket.

I **read** a book.

He **did** good work.
