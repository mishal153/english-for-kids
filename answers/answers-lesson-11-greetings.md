### Exercise 3 Answers | उत्तर

In the morning we say: **Good morning**.

At night before sleep we say: **Good night**.

When leaving we say: **Goodbye**.

When someone helps us we say: **Thank you**.

When we make a mistake we say: **Sorry**.
