### Exercise 3 Answers | उत्तर

The person filling the form is called the **Applicant**.

Information that must be filled is called **Required**.

Information that can be left blank is called **Optional**.

The 6-digit postal code is called **PIN Code**.

Write the name of your city in the **District** column, and your state in the **State** column.
