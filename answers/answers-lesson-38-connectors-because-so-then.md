# Answers — Lesson 38: Connectors because / so / then
## उत्तर — पाठ 37

---

### Exercise 1 Answers | उत्तर

I came late **because** the bus did not come.

It was raining, **so** I stayed home.

I ate food, **then** I slept.

She cried **because** she was scared.

I was hungry, **so** I ate food.

I read the book, **then** I went to sleep.

---

### Exercise 2 Answers | उत्तर

I was tired, **so** I went to sleep.

She passed the exam **because** she studied hard.

I woke up, **then** I brushed my teeth, **then** I ate breakfast.
