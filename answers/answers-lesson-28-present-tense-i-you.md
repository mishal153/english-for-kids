# Answers — Lesson 27: Present Tense I / You
## उत्तर — पाठ 27

---

### Exercise 1 Answers | उत्तर

I **go** to school every day.

I **sleep** at night.

You **play** cricket.

I **read** a book.

You **drink** water.

I **eat** food.

---

### Exercise 2 Answers | उत्तर

Exercise 2 has personal answers. Check that the student:
- Started each sentence with **I**
- Used a verb correctly (eat, drink, go, read, play, sleep, etc.)
- Did not add **-s** to the verb (that is only for he/she/it)
