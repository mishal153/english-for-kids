### Exercise 2 Answers | उत्तर

```
मेरा नाम है         →  My name is
मैं ___ साल का हूँ  →  I am ___ years old
मैं ___ में रहता हूँ →  I live in
मैं कक्षा ___ में हूँ →  I study in class
```

### Exercise 3 Answers | उत्तर

Exercise 3 has personal answers — check that the student used the correct sentence structure:

- My name is **[name]**.
- I am **[age]** years old.
- I live in **[city/place]**.
- I study in class **[number]**.
- My favourite colour is **[colour]**.
- My father's name is **[name]**.
- My date of birth is **[date]**.
- My district is **[district]**.
- My state is **[state]**.
