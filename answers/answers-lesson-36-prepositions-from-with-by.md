# Answers — Lesson 36: Prepositions from / with / by
## उत्तर — पाठ 35

---

### Exercise 1 Answers | उत्तर

```
मैं दिल्ली से हूँ          →  I am from Delhi.
मैं कलम से लिखता हूँ       →  I write with a pen.
मैं बस से आता हूँ          →  I come by bus.
वह अपने दोस्त के साथ आया  →  He came with his friend.
```

### Exercise 2 Answers | उत्तर

I am **from** Mumbai.

I write **with** a pen.

He comes **by** bus.

I went **with** my mother.

I am **from** district Varanasi.

This letter is **from** the government office.
