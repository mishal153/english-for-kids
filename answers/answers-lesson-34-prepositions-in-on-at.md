# Answers — Lesson 34: Prepositions in / on / at
## उत्तर — पाठ 34

---

### Exercise 1 Answers | उत्तर

```
किताब मेज़ पर है     →  The book is on the table.
बिल्ली डिब्बे में है →  The cat is in the box.
मैं स्कूल में हूँ   →  I am at school.
वह सोमवार को आएगा  →  He will come on Monday.
```

### Exercise 2 Answers | उत्तर

The book is **on** the table.

I am **at** school.

It is **in** the box.

I will come **on** Monday.

The party is **at** 5 o'clock.

She lives **in** India.
