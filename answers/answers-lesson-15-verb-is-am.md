# Answers — Lesson 15: Verbs is / am
## उत्तर — पाठ 14

---

### Exercise 2 Answers | उत्तर

I **am** happy.

He **is** tired.

She **is** smart.

It **is** a dog.

I **am** the applicant.

This form **is** required.

---

### Rule Reminder | नियम याद रखो

- Use **am** only with **I**
- Use **is** with **he**, **she**, **it**, and names (Ravi, Priya, the cat...)
