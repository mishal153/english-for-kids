# Lesson 38 — Connectors: because / so / then
## विषय: जोड़ने वाले शब्द — because / so / then

---

## Concept | अवधारणा

| Word | Hindi | Use |
|------|-------|-----|
| **because** | क्योंकि | gives a reason |
| **so** | इसलिए | gives a result |
| **then** | फिर / तब | shows what happened next |

Examples:
- I was sick **because** I ate bad food.
- It was raining, **so** I stayed home.
- I ate, **then** I slept.

---

### Exercise 1: Fill in the Blank | खाली जगह भरो

Use **because**, **so**, or **then**. | सही शब्द लगाओ।

मैं देर से आया क्योंकि बस आई नहीं।
I came late ___________ the bus did not come.


बारिश हो रही थी इसलिए मैं घर रहा।
It was raining, ___________ I stayed home.


मैंने खाना खाया, फिर सो गया।
I ate food, ___________ I slept.


वह रोई क्योंकि वह डरी हुई थी।
She cried ___________ she was scared.


मुझे भूख लगी थी इसलिए मैंने खाना खाया।
I was hungry, ___________ I ate food.


मैंने किताब पढ़ी, फिर सो गया।
I read the book, ___________ I went to sleep.

---

### Exercise 2: Choose the Best Connector | सही शब्द चुनो

I was tired. I went to sleep.
→ I was tired, ___________ I went to sleep. (so / because / then)

She studied hard. She passed the exam.
→ She passed the exam ___________ she studied hard. (so / because / then)

I woke up. I brushed my teeth. I ate breakfast.
→ I woke up, ___________ I brushed my teeth, ___________ I ate breakfast.
