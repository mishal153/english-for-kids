# Lesson 30 — Present Tense: We / They
## विषय: वर्तमान काल — We / They

---

## Concept | अवधारणा

| Hindi | English |
|-------|---------|
| हम खाते हैं | We **eat** |
| वे खेलते हैं | They **play** |

With **we** and **they** → use the verb without **-s** (same as I / You)

---

### Exercise 1: Fill in the Blank | खाली जगह भरो

**(eat / go / play / read / drink / live)**

हम स्कूल जाते हैं।
We ___________ to school.


वे क्रिकेट खेलते हैं।
They ___________ cricket.


हम किताबें पढ़ते हैं।
We ___________ books.


वे पानी पीते हैं।
They ___________ water.


हम यहाँ रहते हैं।
We ___________ here.


वे खाना खाते हैं।
They ___________ food.

---

### Exercise 2: Choose the Correct Verb | सही क्रिया चुनो

My friends (play / plays) football.


We (go / goes) to school together.


They (eat / eats) lunch at noon.


She (read / reads) every night.


We (live / lives) near the market.
