# Lesson 34 — Future Tense: going to
## विषय: भविष्यकाल — going to

---

## Concept | अवधारणा

**going to** = plan that is already decided | जो पहले से तय हो

**Subject + am/is/are + going to + verb**

| Hindi | English |
|-------|---------|
| मैं खाना खाने वाला हूँ | I **am going to** eat |
| वह स्कूल जाने वाली है | She **is going to** go to school |
| हम खेलने वाले हैं | We **are going to** play |

---

### Exercise 1: Fill in the Blank | खाली जगह भरो

Use **am / is / are going to** + verb.

मैं पढ़ने वाला हूँ।
I ___________ ___________ ___________ read.


वह सोने वाला है।
He ___________ ___________ ___________ sleep.


हम खेलने वाले हैं।
We ___________ ___________ ___________ play.


वे खाना खाने वाले हैं।
They ___________ ___________ ___________ eat.


वह गाना गाने वाली है।
She ___________ ___________ ___________ sing.

---

### Exercise 2: Choose will or going to | सही चुनो

I ___________ eat pizza tonight. (planned: going to / will)


If you fall, I ___________ help you. (not planned: will / going to)


She ___________ visit her grandmother on Sunday. (planned)


It looks cloudy. It ___________ rain. (going to — sign is visible)
