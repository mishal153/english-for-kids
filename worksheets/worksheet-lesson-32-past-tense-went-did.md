# Lesson 31 — Past Tense: went / did / ate
## विषय: भूतकाल — गया, किया, खाया

---

### Understand First | पहले समझो

These are irregular past tense verbs. | ये अनियमित भूतकाल क्रियाएँ हैं।

| Present | Past | Hindi |
|---------|------|-------|
| go | **went** | गया |
| do | **did** | किया |
| eat | **ate** | खाया |
| drink | **drank** | पिया |
| read | **read** | पढ़ा |
| play | **played** | खेला |

---

### Exercise 1: Match Present to Past | क्रिया मिलाओ

```
go     •          •   ate
eat    •          •   played
drink  •          •   went
play   •          •   read
read   •          •   drank
```
