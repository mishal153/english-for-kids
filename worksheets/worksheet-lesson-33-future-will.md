# Lesson 32 — Future Tense: will
## विषय: भविष्यकाल — will

---

### Understand First | पहले समझो

Use **will** to talk about the future. | भविष्य की बात करने के लिए **will** लगाते हैं।

**Subject + will + verb**

| Hindi | English |
|-------|---------|
| मैं कल स्कूल जाऊँगा | I **will go** to school tomorrow |
| वह खाना खाएगी | She **will eat** food |
| हम खेलेंगे | We **will play** |

---

### Exercise 1: Fill in the Blank | खाली जगह भरो

Use **will** + correct verb. | **will** + क्रिया लगाओ।

कल मैं स्कूल जाऊँगा।
Tomorrow I ___________ to school.


वह कल आएगी।
She ___________ tomorrow.


हम मिलकर खेलेंगे।
We ___________ together.


वे पानी पिएंगे।
They ___________ water.


मैं किताब पढ़ूँगा।
I ___________ a book.

---

### Exercise 2: Rewrite in Future Tense | भविष्यकाल में लिखो

I eat rice. → Tomorrow I ___________ rice.


She goes to school. → Tomorrow she ___________ to school.


They play cricket. → Tomorrow they ___________ cricket.


He reads a book. → Tomorrow he ___________ a book.
