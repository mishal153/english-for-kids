# Lesson 30 — Past Tense: was / were
## विषय: भूतकाल — was / were

---

### Understand First | पहले समझो

**was** and **were** are the past forms of **is / am / are**

| Present | Past |
|---------|------|
| I am | I **was** |
| He / She / It is | He / She / It **was** |
| You / We / They are | You / We / They **were** |

---

### Exercise 1: Fill in the Blank | खाली जगह भरो

Use **was** or **were**. | **was** या **were** लगाओ।

कल मैं बीमार था।
Yesterday I ___________ sick.


वह कल खुश था।
He ___________ happy yesterday.


हम कल स्कूल में थे।
We ___________ at school yesterday.


वे कल बाहर थे।
They ___________ outside yesterday.


वह लड़की होशियार थी।
She ___________ smart.


आसमान साफ था।
The sky ___________ clear.
