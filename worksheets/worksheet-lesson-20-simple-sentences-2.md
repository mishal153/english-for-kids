# Lesson 20 — Simple Sentences (Part 2)
## विषय: सरल वाक्य — भाग 2

---

### Understand First | पहले समझो

When subject is **he / she / it** → verb gets **-s**
जब कर्ता he / she / it हो → क्रिया में **-s** जोड़ते हैं

| I eat | She eat**s** |
| I play | He play**s** |
| I drink | It drink**s** |

---

### Exercise 1: Rewrite with He/She | वाक्य बदलो

Change "I" to "She". | "I" को "She" में बदलो।

I eat bread. → She ___________ bread.


I drink milk. → She ___________ milk.


I read a book. → She ___________ a book.


I play cricket. → She ___________ cricket.

---
