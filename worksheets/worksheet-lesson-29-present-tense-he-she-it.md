# Lesson 29 — Present Tense: He / She / It
## विषय: वर्तमान काल — He / She / It

---

## Concept | अवधारणा

When subject is **he / she / it** → add **-s** to the verb!
जब कर्ता he / she / it हो → क्रिया में **-s** जोड़ते हैं!

| I eat | → | She eat**s** |
| I go | → | He go**es** |
| I play | → | It play**s** |

---

### Exercise 1: Change the Sentence | वाक्य बदलो

Change "I" to "She". | "I" को "She" में बदलो।

I eat rice.
She ___________ rice.


I go to school.
She ___________ to school.


I play outside.
She ___________ outside.


I read a book.
She ___________ a book.


I drink milk.
She ___________ milk.
